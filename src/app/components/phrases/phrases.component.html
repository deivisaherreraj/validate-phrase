Negative Sentences Present Tense with the Verb To be The sentences are composed: "Subject + Verb To Be + Not + Complement"

As the name implies, this form is used to deny. To form it add the adverb "not" after the verb. It can be clearer with some examples: I am not a good student, She is not absent from class today, He is not a businessman, You are not a lawyer, It is not a sunny day, We are not a couple, They are not married, This motorbike is not expensive, That motorbike is not expensive, These cars are not dirty y Those cars are not dirty.
